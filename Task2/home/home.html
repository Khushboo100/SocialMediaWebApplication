Empower Ananya

ANANYA SCHOLARSHIPS support first generation learners, young women from economically challenged backgrounds studying in STEM particularly tertiary professional education. This is with a focus to ensure meritorious women get into tech jobs.
This series 1 of 2022 has brilliant students who have proven academic proficiency. We are honing their employability skills along with the scholarships.
COVID has been tough and we have many young people who have been hugely impacted by loss of their loved ones, loss of jobs of the primary earning member, or loss of opportunities previously available to make ends meet.
This has a obvious impact on their ability to complete their graduation. We are trying to help as much as we can so we can support them as they try to cope with their losses.
